# E-Commerce-Sales-Customer-Analytics-Dashboard

1. Project Description / Purpose
  The purpose of this project is to analyze e-commerce sales performance, order fulfillment statuses, and customer distributions across     various cities. This dashboard helps business owners track revenue growth, identify top-performing regions, and understand customer       payment preferences to make data-driven decisions.
2. Tech Stack
 •  Business Intelligence Tool:  Microsoft Power BI Desktop
 •	Data Transformation:  Power Query (Data Cleaning & Modeling)
 •	Design:  Custom UI/UX Optimization (Dark Theme Template)
3. Data Source
    Dataset: E-Commerce Transactions & Customer Data (CSV/Excel format containing sales figures, customer locations like Sao Paulo, and       order details).
4. Features and Highlights                                                                                                                 •  Key KPI Cards:  Tracks Total Revenue ($366.41K), Total Orders (3K), and Average Order Value ($122.14) dynamically.
 •  Geographical Analysis: Highlighting the Top 5 Cities (including Sao Paulo, Rio de Janeiro, and Belo Horizonte) by customer                concentration.
 •	Sales Trends: Monthly Revenue Trend visual tracking performance across months (January to December).
 •	Operational Insights:  Donut chart analyzing Order Status Distribution (Delivered, Shipped, Canceled).
 •	Payment Insights: Bar chart breakdown of Payment Method Distribution (Credit Card, Boleto, Voucher, Debit Card).
 •	Interactive Controls: Modern, space-saving Dropdown Slicers with clean visual borders for seamless data filtering.
5. Business Conclusion
 •	Geographical Targeting: Sao Paulo and Rio de Janeiro are the biggest revenue drivers. The business should allocate 60% of its             marketing budget to these top5 cities to maximize Return on Investment (ROI).
 •	Payment Optimization: Since Credit Card is the highly preferred payment method, introducing exclusive bank cashback offers or No-Cost     EMI options can help increase the Average Order Value ($122.14) even further.
 •	Operational Efficiency: The donut chart shows a healthy delivery rate. However, keeping a close check on the Canceled and Shipped         percentages will ensure better customer retention and reduce logistical losses.
6. Why This Dashboard is Useful?
 •	Centralized Data (Single Source of Truth): Instead of digging through multiple messy Excel sheets, business owners can monitor sales,     operations, and customer metrics all in one single screen.
 •	Actionable Decision Making: With interactive dropdown slicers, management can filter the data by a specific city (e.g., Sao Paulo) to    understand local payment preferences and launch customized region-specific campaigns.
 •	Inventory & Demand Forecasting: The Monthly Revenue Trend allows stakeholders to identify seasonal spikes, helping the operations         team stock up on inventory well before peak sales months.
